Charity Events

The Take That Experience

The Take That Experience’ – This is an annually held event which has been running for 3 years in order to raise funds for the foundation. Held at The Floral Pavilion in New Brighton, this event has gone from strength to strength each year. The event begins with opening acts consisting of local and nationally recognised talents, including Britain’s’ Got Talents’ James Smith, Liverpool’s Sophia Ellen, and Nicholls and- Moroney amongst others. This year’s event was hosted by Russell Grant, who works closely with Dementia UK. After the interval The Take That Experience take to the stage, providing fans with a realistic experience of Take That. In total, more than £5000 this year.

Black and White Ball

A brand new event for the Foundation. Guests are invited to attend for a luxurious three course meal and evening of entertainment at the fabulous Thornton Hall Hotel on the Wirral. Live acts will perform throughout the night with a Prize Auction mid-way through and a late night bar and DJ to finish. This event is a first for the Foundation. Held at Thornton Hall Hotel, a magnificent 3 course meal and drinks reception will be held. This will be followed by a glamourous prize auction, with special guest to be announced. We hope everyone has a fabulous night and raise lots of monies for the benefit of local people living with Dementia.
